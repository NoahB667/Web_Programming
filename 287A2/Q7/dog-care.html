How to Take Good Care of Your Dog

1. Purchase a brand of food with high quality ingredients.

2. Feed your dog on a regular schedule.

3. Monitor your dog's appetite and eating habits.

4. Provide water at all times.

5. Make sure your dog gets plenty of exercises.
