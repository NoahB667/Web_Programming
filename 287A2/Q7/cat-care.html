How to Take Good Care of Your Cat

1. Encourage the cat to use a litter box.

2. Make the litter box a comfortable place.

3. Take young or old cats needs into consideration.

4. Provide the cat with a scratching post.

5. Discourage the cat from exploring forbidden surfaces.

6. Consider using feline pheromone products.
